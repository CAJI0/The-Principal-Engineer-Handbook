# Successful Prototype

## Definition

A prototype that worked well enough to become production architecture without review.

## Why It Matters

Prototype decisions often preserve assumptions that were never meant to last.

## Symptoms

- Fast paths become permanent.
- Test gaps are accepted because the prototype proved the idea.
- Product variants strain early shortcuts.

## Example

A demo firmware architecture ships because it passed customer validation before ownership boundaries were reviewed.
