# RFC Friday

## Purpose

Create regular review space for proposals before they become implementation facts.

## Cadence

Weekly or biweekly.

## Participants

RFC authors, reviewers from affected areas, and engineers who will maintain the outcome.

## Process

Review active RFCs, clarify open questions, and decide next evidence or decision steps.

## Output

Updated RFCs, accepted decisions, rejected proposals, or scheduled deeper reviews.

## Failure Modes

The ritual becomes too broad, too ceremonial, or disconnected from actual decisions.
