# Architecture Court

## Purpose

Resolve contested architecture decisions with evidence and explicit trade-offs.

## Cadence

Use only when normal review cannot resolve a decision.

## Participants

Decision owners, affected stakeholders, senior technical reviewers, and one accountable decision maker.

## Process

Present context, alternatives, evidence, risks, and consequences; record the final decision.

## Output

An accepted ADR and follow-up actions.

## Failure Modes

The ritual becomes politics, authority theater, or a substitute for earlier review.
