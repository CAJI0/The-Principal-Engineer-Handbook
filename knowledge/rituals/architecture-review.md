# Architecture Review

## Purpose

Review decisions before they become expensive to change.

## Cadence

Run before major implementation, release gates, or irreversible integration.

## Participants

Decision owner, affected engineers, reviewers from adjacent boundaries, and a facilitator when useful.

## Process

Review context, constraints, alternatives, risks, evidence, and open questions.

## Output

An ADR, RFC update, decision journal entry, or explicit rejection.

## Failure Modes

The review becomes a presentation, happens too late, or avoids trade-offs.
