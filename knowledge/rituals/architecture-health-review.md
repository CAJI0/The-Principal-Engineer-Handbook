# Architecture Health Review

## Purpose

Assess whether architecture still supports necessary change.

## Cadence

Run periodically and after major incidents or releases.

## Participants

Principal engineers, module owners, test leads, release owners, and product-facing technical leads.

## Process

Review change radius, weak signals, defects, ownership gaps, and stale decisions.

## Output

Health notes, follow-up ADRs, deletion candidates, and review triggers.

## Failure Modes

The review becomes status reporting or avoids uncomfortable ownership questions.
