# Unused Flexibility Is Waste

## Statement

Flexibility that is not used or justified becomes maintenance cost.

## Meaning

Options are valuable only when they protect against real uncertainty.

## Why It Matters

Unused flexibility increases test space, review cost, and cognitive load.

## Typical Violation

A driver supports five configuration modes even though the product has only one validated mode.

## Recovery

Remove unused paths or document the uncertainty that justifies keeping them.
