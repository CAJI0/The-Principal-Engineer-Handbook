# Evidence Before Confidence

## Statement

Confidence should follow evidence, not replace it.

## Meaning

Engineering judgment improves when assumptions are tested, observed, or recorded.

## Why It Matters

Unverified confidence turns guesses into architecture.

## Typical Violation

A team accepts a timing margin because the prototype "never failed on the bench."

## Recovery

Identify the assumption, gather evidence, record confidence, and set a review trigger.
