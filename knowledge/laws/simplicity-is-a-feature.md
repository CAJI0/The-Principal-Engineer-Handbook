# Simplicity Is a Feature

## Statement

Simplicity is a product capability because it makes future change safer.

## Meaning

A simple system is easier to review, operate, test, and explain.

## Why It Matters

Complexity consumes engineering capacity even when behavior appears correct.

## Typical Violation

A product keeps legacy paths, optional modes, and unused hooks because each one seems harmless alone.

## Recovery

Delete unused behavior, narrow interfaces, and make ownership explicit.
