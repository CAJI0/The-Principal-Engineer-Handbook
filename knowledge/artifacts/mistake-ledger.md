# Mistake Ledger

## Purpose

Preserve lessons from failures as reusable engineering memory.

## When To Use

Use it after incidents, escaped defects, delayed releases, or repeated review failures.

## Template

- What happened
- What assumption failed
- Why detection missed it
- What changed
- Review trigger

## Common Mistakes

- Blaming a person instead of identifying the system condition.
- Recording a fix without the missed assumption.
- Letting entries become long postmortems.
