# ADR

## Purpose

Record an architectural decision, why it was made, and what consequences it creates.

## When To Use

Use an ADR when a decision will affect future architecture, ownership, cost, or reversibility.

## Template

- Status
- Context
- Decision
- Consequences
- Alternatives considered

## Common Mistakes

- Recording only the chosen option.
- Hiding uncertainty.
- Writing ADRs after everyone has forgotten the real trade-off.
