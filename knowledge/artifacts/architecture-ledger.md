# Architecture Ledger

## Purpose

Maintain a compact inventory of active architectural decisions and known debts.

## When To Use

Use it when a system has many decisions spread across ADRs, RFCs, and team memory.

## Template

- Decision
- Owner
- Status
- Related artifact
- Review date

## Common Mistakes

- Turning the ledger into duplicate documentation.
- Listing everything instead of decisions that still affect change.
- Leaving owner fields blank.
