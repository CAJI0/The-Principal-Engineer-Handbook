# Decision Journal

## Purpose

Capture decision reasoning when a full ADR would be too heavy.

## When To Use

Use it for reversible or medium-sized decisions where evidence and review triggers still matter.

## Template

- Date
- Decision
- Evidence
- Confidence
- Review trigger

## Common Mistakes

- Recording opinion without evidence.
- Forgetting the review trigger.
- Mixing unrelated decisions in one entry.
