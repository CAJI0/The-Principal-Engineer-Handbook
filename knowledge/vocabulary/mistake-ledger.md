# Mistake Ledger

## Definition

A record of engineering mistakes and the system conditions that made them possible.

## Why It Matters

It turns failure into reusable memory without blaming individuals.

## Symptoms

- The same class of defect returns.
- Postmortems produce actions but no reusable pattern.
- Teams remember pain but not prevention.

## Example

A release failure is logged with the missed assumption, detection gap, and future review trigger.
