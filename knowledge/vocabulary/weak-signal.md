# Weak Signal

## Definition

An early, low-confidence sign that a system decision may be aging badly.

## Why It Matters

Weak signals give teams time to investigate before a pattern becomes expensive.

## Symptoms

- Repeated small exceptions.
- Review comments that keep returning.
- Workarounds that are not yet treated as defects.

## Example

Multiple teams add local timeouts around the same driver, but no one has opened a design review yet.
