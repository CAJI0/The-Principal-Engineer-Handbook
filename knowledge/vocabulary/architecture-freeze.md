# Architecture Freeze

## Definition

A temporary pause on architectural change while a team stabilizes a product or decision.

## Why It Matters

It prevents late structural churn from hiding release risk.

## Symptoms

- Design changes continue after validation begins.
- Teams cannot tell whether a failure is new or pre-existing.
- Release readiness keeps moving.

## Example

Before field validation, a team freezes interface changes except for approved defect fixes.
