# Deletion Day

## Definition

A planned ritual for removing unused code, stale options, dead configuration, and obsolete architecture.

## Why It Matters

Deletion protects simplicity and reduces future change cost.

## Symptoms

- Unused feature flags remain active.
- Dead paths still need tests.
- Engineers avoid changes because old behavior might matter.

## Example

A product team schedules one day per release to remove obsolete manufacturing modes.
