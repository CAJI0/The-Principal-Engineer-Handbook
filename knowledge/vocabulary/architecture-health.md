# Architecture Health

## Definition

The current ability of a system to absorb necessary change without disproportionate cost.

## Why It Matters

Architecture health makes long-term maintainability discussable.

## Symptoms

- Small changes require broad coordination.
- Engineers avoid certain modules.
- Decisions are hard to discover.

## Example

A firmware platform is considered unhealthy when every product variant requires special-case changes.
