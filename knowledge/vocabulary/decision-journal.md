# Decision Journal

## Definition

A lightweight record of decisions, evidence, confidence, and review triggers.

## Why It Matters

It preserves reasoning before memory turns into folklore.

## Symptoms

- Teams remember the decision but not why it was made.
- Old trade-offs are treated as permanent truths.
- Reversing a decision feels risky because evidence is missing.

## Example

A team records why a cheaper flash part was accepted and when the decision must be reviewed.
