# Style Guide

## Rules

- Use clear English.
- Avoid generic advice.
- Every principle needs context, trade-off, and consequence.
- Avoid "best practice" unless explained.
- Avoid "always" and "never" unless the exception is discussed.
- Prefer stories, design reviews, ADRs, and concrete artifacts.
- Code appears only when it clarifies an architectural decision.
- No filler text.
- No motivational fluff without engineering value.

## Chapter Architecture

Every major chapter must follow `editor/CHAPTER_ARCHITECTURE.md`.

Use `templates/chapter-template.md` when creating chapter drafts.

Do not create a full chapter until the story, principle, exercise, and ADR are known.

## Maintainer Boundary

Maintainers may organize, refactor, format, cross-reference, and improve maintainability. Maintainers must not invent manuscript content.
